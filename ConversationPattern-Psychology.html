Conversation Pattern

1
Intro sentence
Start the conversation with a general introduction to the topic to set context and capture attention.
2
The main advantage is that...
Present the main positive aspect or key benefit that is most important to the speaker.
3
In addition, ...
Add supporting information that strengthens the main advantage or provides additional details.
4
However, ...
Discuss possible limitations, obstacles, or alternative perspectives to demonstrate objectivity.
5
And another thing, ...
Present an additional argument or clarification that is important but didn't fit into previous points.
6
In general, ...
Summarize the discussion, recap key points, and formulate an overall conclusion or recommendation.
